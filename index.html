Synopsis

Tserof Sutef《胎動森林》 is an interactive sound game focused on the grip of identity experienced by today’s Chinese youth. It is a continuous conversation of self-exploration. Its elements are distilled from collective generational memories, subjective emotional states, and everyday social realities, forming an ever-evolving dialogue between external structures and the inner self in pursuit of authenticity.
There are three chapters/worlds in Tserof Sutef. The three symbolic virtual worlds are sequentially connected. Each world is a consequence of the previous one which questions the players: how can one always make a honest choice within a constrained society? Tserof Sutef emphasizes the players journey as an continuous process without a fixed beginning or ending, making the worlds a complete feedback loop.
